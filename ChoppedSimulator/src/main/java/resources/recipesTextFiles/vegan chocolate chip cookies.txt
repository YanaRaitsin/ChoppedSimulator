kitchen entities: spatula, tray, bowl, tea spoon, spoon.
1. In a large bowl whisk together the coconut oil, brown sugar, and vanilla, beating until well combined. Add in the coconut milk and applesauce and whisk until well combined; set aside. In a separate bowl combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; whisk well to combine.
2. Add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and, using a wooden spoon or very sturdy spatula, stir until ingredients are combined. 
3. Bake for 9 to 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the centers have set. (bake in the oven)
