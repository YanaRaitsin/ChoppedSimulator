kitchen entities: spatula, tray, bowl, tea spoon, spoon.
1. Beat egg in a large bowl, add cashew meal and spices into the bowl and than chicken breast.
2. Dip chicken breast into egg with left hand and place into cashew meal mixture and mix to coat with right hand. (add the combined ingredients on tray).
3. Bake for 20 minutes. (bake in the oven)
