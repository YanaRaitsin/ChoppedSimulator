kitchen entities: spatula, bowl, tea spoon, spoon, blender, tray.
1. To a large bowl add creamy almond butter, palm sugar, maple syrup or honey, vanilla extract, and eggs. Mix well until mixture is fully combined and thick. (add ingredients into bowl)
2. process cacao powder, coconut flour, baking soda, and sea salt until combined. Finally, stir in dark chocolate chips until just combined.
3. Scoop brownie mixture into greased baking dish. You may need to use a wet spatula to smooth our thick mixture in the pan. (add combined ingredients on tray)
4. Bake brownies for 20-25 minutes, or until edges are slightly browned and knife inserted into the center comes out clean. (bake in the oven)
