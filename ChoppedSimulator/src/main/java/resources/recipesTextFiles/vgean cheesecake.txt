kitchen entities: spatula, tray, bowl, tea spoon, spoon, blender.
1. Add oats, almonds, sea salt, and coconut sugar to a high speed blender and mix on high until a fine meal is achieved. (process in the blender oats, almonds, sea salt, coconut sugar).
2. Remove lid and add melted coconut oil, starting with 4 Tbsp. (in the bowl with combined ingredients, add coconut oil).
3. Add cashews are soaked and drained, add to a high speed blender with coconut cream, vegan cream cheese, cornstarch, vanilla, organic maple syrup, coconut oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, and sea salt. Blend on high until very creamy and smooth. (process in the blender cashews, coconut cream, vegan cream cheese, cornstarch, vanilla, organic maple syrup, coconut oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, sea salt)
5. Pour filling over the pre-baked crust and spread into an even layer. Tap on counter to remove air bubbles. (add comnbined ingredients into the tray)
6. Bake for 50 minutes to 1 hour, until the edges look very slightly dry and the center appears only slightly “jiggly” but not liquidy. (bake in the oven)
