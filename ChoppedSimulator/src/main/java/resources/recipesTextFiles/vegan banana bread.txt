kitchen entities: spatula, tray, bowl, tea spoon, spoon.
1. Mash 3 large black peeled bananas with a fork, then mix well with 75g vegetable or sunflower oil and 100g brown sugar.
2. Add 225g plain flour, 3 heaped tsp baking powder and 3 tsp cinnamon or mixed spice, and combine well.
3. Bake in a greased, lined 2lb loaf tin for 20 minutes. (bake in the oven)
