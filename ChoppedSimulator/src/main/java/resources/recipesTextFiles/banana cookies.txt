1. In a large bowl, mash the bananas. Stir in oats, dates, oil, and vanilla. Mix well, and allow to sit for 15 minutes. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
2. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until lightly brown. (bake in the oven)
